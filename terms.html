Important Terms

CC
CC , or Crowd Control, is any effect that prevents someone from doing something. This includes roots/stuns, which prevent moving, disarms which prevent shooting, and silences, which prevent ability usage.
Spirit Jar
The Spirit Jar, more commonly referred to as the "Urn" is an objective that spawns at 10 minutes, then every 5 minutes after that. Players can pick up the urn when it spawns and take it across the map for bonus souls for their team, with the urn runner getting a few extra small bonuses. The urn has a comeback mechanic, where if a team is behind by 15&percnt for the first urn and 10&percnt for every urn after that, the dropoff point will be favored for that team, regardless of who picks up the urn
