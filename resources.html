Content Creators To Watch

Deathy
Deathy is the face of the Deadlock community. He makes a lot of great educational content for players of all skill levels. He also streams regularly, and is always happy to answer questions. You can also book a coaching session with him if you want one on one lessons. He also regularly hosts community tournaments with several brackets so players of all levels can get experience playing in a team environment.
Deadlock Night Shift
This is the home of the biggest professional tournaments in Deadlock. Deadlock Night Shift is a weekly tournament for the best teams from each region to compete every Wednesday. This is a great resource to find the current meta, as well as to watch some of the best high-level gameplay available.
Natan
Natan makes educational content for Deadlock, thoguh not quite to the same degree as Deathy.
Midknight
Midknight does regular, free coaching for his community, uploading videos of his coaching sessions frequently. This is a great resource for players of all skill levels to learn.
Poshypop
Poshypop is a top player for Floormen who makes educational content.
Xera
Xera is a high level player who uploads and streams frequently.
Eido
Eido is arguably the best player in North America. He streams both matchmaking and competitive games with his team frequently.
Zerggy
Zerggy is a high level player who streams daily, uploading content very often as well.
Datto
Though not the best player, Datto makes very solid educational content.

Other Resources

Statlocker
Statlocker is a fantastic resource for all kinds of statistics. It hosts data from all matches of Deadlock that are played. You can find the profiles of any player and view their previous matches. This includes information on their builds and match IDs, so you can watch the match in game. There are a ton of other great tools on Statlocker, so go and take a look.
Deadlock Meta
This is Deathy's website with his tierlist, as well as recommended builds for every hero in the game. A fantastic resource if you need a build, though take his rankings with a grain of salt.
Death Slam Academy
Death Slam Academy is a place to get premium lessons from high level players. These lessons often contain fantastic information, though I can only really recommend them for dedicated players.
